THE STARTUP CHALLENEGE

This Startup Idea Contest has been specially crafted to give a chance to the innovative IDEAS. Do you have an innovative idea? Participate in one of its kind competition for Entrepreneurs. Compete with the like-minded people where a lot of prizes.
Register here !

CONTEST

  • You will have to prepare a presentation of your startup idea.
  • You can register to the contest here
  • The best idea/presentation among the participants will be rewarded with cash prize.
  • Every participant will receive a 'Participation Certificate'.

    • GUIDELINES

  • Each team should consist of a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 4 members.
  • All submissions to be submitted using the provided platform - Google Meet.
  • The Presentation should broadly address the problem targeted, how it would be resolved, who all will benefit, and how you will reach the first customer; any prototype or Minimum Viable Product could also be displayed.
  • While presenting the Idea, the funding requirements, current and projected financials, break-up of funds, and scaling-up strategy may be explained.
  • Judges’ decisions will be final.

    • RULES

  • There shouldn't be any common members between two Teams
  • Presentation should last no more than fifteen minutes.
  • Should contain no font smaller than thirty points.
  • Should be very visual and contain very little text.
  • Only Team Leader will be able to make the submission.
  • Your power point presentation should have ten slides.
  • You must include - Title, Problem/Opportunity, Market Size, Business Model (Your Solution) in you presentation.
  • Each team may enter only one idea.
  • The Judging Panel reserves the right to disqualify any entry.
  • A single individual cannot be a member of more than one team.
